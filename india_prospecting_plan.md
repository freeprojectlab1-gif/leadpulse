## 🚀 Mastering the Map Finder

1.  **Industrial Scan**: Search `Manufacturer` in `Ankleshwar` or `Vapi`.
2.  **Premium Scan**: Search `Architect` in `South Mumbai` or `Gurgaon`.
3.  **Educational Scan**: Search `Coaching` in `Kota` or `Hyderabad`.
4.  **Bulk Scan**: Use "No-website businesses only" to find clients who NEED digital presence.

*Strategy: Target businesses with Google Ratings but NO website first – they are your easiest clients.*
