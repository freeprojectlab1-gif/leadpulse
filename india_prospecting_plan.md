## 🚀 Map Finder — Quick Scan Guide

1. **Driving School** → `driving school` in any city → Easy close, ₹8K–₹20K
2. **Banquet Hall** → `banquet hall` in Tier-2 city → ₹15K–₹50K
3. **Pathology Lab** → `pathology lab` → ₹10K–₹30K
4. **Pest Control** → `pest control` in Mumbai/Delhi → Quick close
5. **Coaching** → `coaching institute` in Kota/Hyderabad → ₹15K–₹40K
6. **Manufacturer** → `plastic manufacturer` in Surat/Rajkot → B2B ₹20K+

> 💡 **Strategy**: Google pe dhoondha, Google Rating hai par website nahi — **yahi tumhara ideal client hai.** Uski aukat hai, sirf kisi ne bataya nahi.
