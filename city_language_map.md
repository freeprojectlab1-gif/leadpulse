### 💡 Pro Tips for LeadPulse Users:

1. **North & West India (DL, PB, RJ, MP, UP, GJ, MH)**:
   * **Hinglish** use karein. Ye "Salesy" nahi lagta balki "Friendly" lagta hai.
   * Example: *"Hi, maine aapka business Google Maps pe dekha, aapka koi website nahi mila..."*

2. **South India (TN, KL, KA, AP)**:
   * **Sirf Pure English** use karein.
   * Yahan Hinglish likhne se wo "Spam" lagta hai ya logo ko samajh nahi aata.
   * Example: *"Hi, I came across {{Business}} on Google Maps and noticed you don't have a website..."*

3. **Hyderabad Exception**:
   * Hyderabad South mein hai par yahan log **Hindi/Urdu** ke sath bahut comfortable hain. Aap yahan Hinglish bindas use kar sakte hain.

4. **West Bengal & East**:
   * Kolkata jaise cities mein English prefer karein, par Hinglish bhi chalti hai.

---

> **Final Thumb Rule**: Agar confusion ho, toh **English** use karein. Wo poore India mein "Professional" mani jati hai. Lekin agar personal touch dena ho, toh upar waale map ke hisaab se language select karein.
