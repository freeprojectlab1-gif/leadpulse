## 🛠️ Lead Qualification Checklist
Check these before sending an email:
1. **Website Status**: Does the "Website" button on Maps lead to a broken link or a Facebook page? (Perfect Lead!)
2. **Menu**: Is the menu just a blurry photo? (Offer Digital Menu/QR Code)
3. **Photos**: Are there no professional food photos? (Offer Content Creation/Website)
4. **Reviews**: Are they losing customers due to bad online management? (Offer Reputation Management)
